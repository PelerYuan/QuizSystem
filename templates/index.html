Quiz System

An interactive platform for online assessments

Welcome to the Quiz System! This platform allows you to take quizzes assigned by your teachers.

For Students
Ready to take a quiz?

You'll need a quiz link from your teacher to access your assessment. The link will direct you to a login page where you can enter your name before starting the quiz.

Once you've completed the quiz, you'll receive immediate feedback on your performance.

For Teachers
Manage your quizzes

Teachers can create and manage quizzes through the admin panel. You can generate unique links for each assessment session.

Admin Login

Important Note!

Students must obtain a quiz link from their teacher before they can access any assessments. If you haven't received a link, please contact your teacher.
